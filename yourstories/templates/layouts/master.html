About This

We want to create a comprehensive archive of how the referendum result and the potential Brexit impacts people, and how this result came into being.

We need more. We are looking for stories about how Boris Johonson or the Brexit in general affected you negatively, as well as ways in which he may have misled the public into voting to leave the European Union. Over the coming days and weeks, we will be working on a way for you to submit your own personal post-Brexit story.
